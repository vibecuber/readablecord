__**Wave 24 East Dinhs**__

> :warning: This dinhs is only relevant for **2x or 4x dinhs** setups. If you are running 3x dinhs, skip this channel.

---
## Wave 22 Dim — Read First

Before approaching the 24 east dinhs, make sure you are aware of the **wave 22 expiry and dim**:
- See: #what-are-dims-and-expiries
- **Do not dinhs dimmed wave 22 crabs** — these are bait dinhs targets

---
## Setup & Rotation

```
Ayak 24 > ayak > ayak (optional 3rd ayak) > dinhs the 24 big
```

**Critical timing rule:**
- Do **not** scythe or dinhs the 24 big without **at least 1 ayak** between the 24 mage big prefire and the 24 melee big kill
- Skipping this will cause the 24 melee big to split before wave 26 spawns — which is when a large number of dimmed crabs expire

---

**Positioning:**
- Dinhs either **north or south** of the west-facing side of the big, depending on where the majority of **undimmed** melee splits are

> The wave 20 east big splits perfectly for this dinhs if the rotation above is followed correctly
