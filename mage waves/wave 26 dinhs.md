__**Wave 26 Dinhs**__

---
## Purpose

The 26 dinhs serves two goals:

**Primary:** Push stall 28 into wave 29 spawn
```
More experienced  → push 2-stall 28 into wave 29 spawn
Less experienced  → push 3-stall 28 into wave 29 spawn
```

**Secondary:** Generate splits early into cleanup
> :warning: These splits will **not** contribute to cleanup if you stall 4 or more 28s — stalling 4+ after wave 26 is a sign of mistakes

Common mistakes for not pushing 29 fast enough are covered in #wave-29-push 

---
## Setup

This is largely room state dependent — a general version is covered here, with the complex version in #wave-29-push

- In general, kill the **west 27 mage big in lane** — it will split into your 26 dinhs
> Killing it out of lane is debatable and entirely room state dependent

- After deciding how many bigs to kill, prioritize smalls in this order while waiting for the **wave 28 small melee** to come in range:
```
1. North melees
2. South mages
3. North mages
```

> When hitting smalls, make sure you are clicking **s27s or higher** — see #dims-and-expiries-overview-unfinished for why this matters
‎

## Performing the 26 Dinhs

- Always dinhs off the **26 big** with your main hitsplat on the big
- Depending on whether there are melee crabs on the southeast pillar, dinhs from either:
  - The **north-facing** side of the big
  - The **east-facing** side of the big

---

**Timing Method 1 — Visual Reference**
- Your exp drop should fire the tick the **wave 28s on the west and east side line up with the barrier**
- Playing with **true tile** on your melee nylos makes this easier
- Click the 26 big when the true tile of the west crabs is directly on the barrier

## Post-26 Dinhs Small Rotation

Immediately after the 26 dinhs:

**Step 1:**  Immediately melee the **east wave 28 melee small** on the southeast pillar
- If the dinhs was **very good** → you can hit a big to generate splits faster

**Step 2:** After hitting exactly **1 crab**, afk for 1 tick in front of the 26's corpse *(if it died)* and be ready to 1-tick whatever split comes out of it
- Stay in **melee gear** with a 2-way switch to react to mages

**If the 26 melee big didn't die:**
- Be aware of this and position in front of the **wave 23 big's corpse** instead, which splits **2 ticks** after the 26 would have split
- This is on the **northeast pillar** *(see screenshot above)*
