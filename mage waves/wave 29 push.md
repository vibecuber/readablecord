__**Waves 26-29 — Complex Room State & 29 Push**__

The goal when pushing 29 is **0 stalls waves 20-27** with **2 or 3 wave 28 stalls**

It is important to have a good understanding of all of the following to improve your 29 pushes:
- #transition-unfinished-approved 
- #wave-19-21 
- #wave-22-dinhs 
- #wave-26-dinhs 
- #cleanup

---
## The 24-26 Observational Period

Use the time spent scything 24-25 and maging the 26 aggro as an **observational period** to scan the room and assess the room state before wave 28 spawns.

**Good Room State Indicators:**
- Killed a lot of bigs on transition
- A lot of south melees available for the 26 dinhs
- 1-shotting bigs 24-26
- A lot of rangers spawned *(room feels generally empty)*
- Killed 1-2 smalls between bigs 24-26
- Valuable barrages mixed with bigs *(barraging two s24/s25/s26 alone is not a good barrage — see #dims-and-expiries-overview-unfinished )*

**Bad Room State Indicators:**
- Didn't kill many bigs on transition
- Single mage-heavy room
- Falling behind on bigs 24-26
- Few rangers spawned *(room full of smalls)*
- No time to hit smalls between bigs

> An average room state will typically be a 50/50 mix of good and bad indicators

## Step-by-Step Rotation
*(All of steps 1-3 happen between waves 26-27, before wave 28 spawns)*

---
**Step 1 — Kill the 26 West Mage Aggro**

By this point you should have already assessed your room state and planned your next actions. If you're struggling to read room state, rewatch your runs and compare them against the indicators above to build better observational habits.

---

**Step 2 — To Pop or Not to Pop the 27 West Mage Big**

```
Average/Good room state → Kill the 27 west mage big (Step 2A)
Bad room state          → Skip the 27 west mage big (Step 2B)
```

**Step 2A — Killing the 27 big:**
- Kill it **in lane** so the splits come out in time for the 26 dinhs
- Do **not** spend more than 2 ayaks on it — more than 2 means it won't split in time

**Step 2B — Skipping the 27 big:**
```
Really behind on aggros → Go straight into the 26 dinhs
Ahead on aggros but room is full → Kill smalls instead of popping another big
```

> If you can't yet tell the difference between average and bad room states, it is always safer to kill **fewer bigs** rather than more

---

**Step 3A — Extra Bigs on Good Room State**

- Kill the **west 27 big** if the room is good
- Do **not** spend more than **1 ayak** on this big — it won't split in time for a 2-stall 28 if you do
> If you don't care about 2-stall 28, putting 2 ayaks into is fine on a very good room state
- Popping more than 2 bigs total requires strong observational skill to distinguish good from average room states

**Step 3B — Average/Bad Room State**

Repeat Step 2B:
```
Really behind on aggros → Go straight into the 26 dinhs
Ahead on aggros but room is full → Kill smalls instead
```
