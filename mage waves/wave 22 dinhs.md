__**Wave 22 Dinhs**__

This channel covers multiple approaches to getting a better wave 22 dinhs. Before anything else, make sure #wave-19-21 is fully memorized — it directly impacts the quality of your 22 dinhs.

This channel picks up from after: `Barrage 21 > mac/scythe`

---
## The Single Most Important 22 Dinhs Prep

These two rules carry over from 19-21 and must be maintained:
- When hitting **mage splits** → mage **south**
- When hitting **melee splits** → melee **north**

---
## Barrages

- Take **south barrages** as soon as possible before the south 21s reach the south pillars
- Take **north barrages** whenever possible
- Barrages are **always** a better use of time than clearing for a dinhs

**Barrage rules:**
```
✓ Barraging two crabs to null 1 crab → okay
✗ Barraging two crabs to null 2 crabs → not okay
```

---
## Killing One vs Two 22 Bigs

While maging before the 22 dinhs, make a conscious decision based on how well transition went:

```
Good transition  → Dinhs on the west side  → Pop both 22 bigs
Poor transition  → Dinhs on the east tile  → Pop one 22 big only
```

> Popping both 22 bigs every single time will variably cause stalls on waves 24-27

---
## Late Dinhs

Late dinhs has a lot of potential and is especially useful when there are still mages in your dinhs AOE by the time you'd normally fire it.

**Key things to know:**
- The 22 dinhs will not clear any melees before the wave 23 check — so being exactly on time is not a huge priority
- There are **17 ticks** between the barrage and late dinhs timing
