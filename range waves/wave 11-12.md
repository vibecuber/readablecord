__**Wave 11 Mage Big & Wave 12 Dinhs Setup**__

---
## Universal Rule

**Objective:** Pop the wave 11 mage big so that it splits **after wave 13 spawns**, regardless of whether you're doing the 12 dinhs or not.

> Simply pop the wave 11 mage big any time **after wave 12 spawns** to achieve this timing

**Room state Exception:**
You may pop it early if both of the following are true:
```
✓ The mager had a very good wave 11
✓ You had a very good wave 10
```
Popping early is only safe when there is no risk of stalling wave 12 by doing so.

---
## Without Wave 12 Dinhs

Before hitting the wave 11 big, prioritize in this order:
```
1. All range smalls
2. All mage smalls s10 or higher
```
> Sometimes this means you won't hit the wave 11 big at all — that's fine

---
## With Wave 12 Dinhs

Before wave 12 spawns:
```
1. All rangers
2. All mages s10 or higher
```

Once wave 12 spawns, position yourself **in the middle of the room on the northern line parallel to the barrier** to pop the mage big
