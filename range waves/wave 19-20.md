__**Waves 19-20 — Transition**__

This channel focuses on achieving an optimal transition into post-cap. Popping bigs is the key to a good post-cap — see #transition-unfinished-approved for full context.

---
## 1 Crab Alive Before Wave 20 Spawns

- The remaining crab **does not need to be killed** to make the check unless you stalled **3 or more times** after wave 15
- It is rare that this extra crab will cause a wave 21 stall if you execute the appropriate 20-21 rotation

**What to do instead:**
- Ayak the **19 west** 2 ticks late after it spawns *(or 3 ticks late with thrall up)*
- After maging 19 west once, proceed as normal and kill your **wave 18 range bigs**
- Ayak **south 19** if it's alive and you have enough ticks after killing the 18s
- If the mager 1-shots the 19 south and you have no splits to hit before the wave 20 prefire → ayak **19 west** if it's still alive

---
## 0 Crabs Alive Before Wave 19 Spawns

**If very far ahead:**
- Tbow the wave 18 big *(before it flickers in lane)* → ayak the **19 mage big** 2-3 ticks late *(depending on thrall)* → kill your wave 18 range bigs

**If slightly ahead** *(too many smalls to prefire the 18 range big, but nothing to hit when the 18 bigs enter the room)*:
- Instantly kill the wave 18 range big the tick it enters the room

**If you 1-shot the west wave 18 big:**
- Ayak **west 19 mage** 2-3 ticks late *(depending on thrall)*
- Do **not** instantly go for the second wave 18 big after 1-shotting the first — if you also 1-shot the second big it will cause a stall

---

**Stall Thresholds — Smalls Alive When an 18 Big Dies:**
```
✓ 2 smalls alive after 1 wave 18 big dies     → safe
✓ 1 small alive with all wave 18 bigs alive   → safe
✗ 3 smalls alive with 1 wave 18 big dead      → stall
```

> The wave 18 big splitting before wave 20 spawns will **not** cause a stall as long as there are **0 extra smalls** alive when it splits
