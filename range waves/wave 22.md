__**Wave 22**__

This channel covers the optional wave 22 prefire and how to set your mager up for a good 22 dinhs. This channel picks up immediately after the rotation in #wave-20-21 

---
## Priorities

Listed in order of importance:

```
1. South rangers
2. North rangers
3. South east mages    (only if 2 or fewer are alive before dinhs)
4. South west mages    (only if 2 or fewer are alive before dinhs)
5. The 22 west prefire (only if none of the above are present)
```

**Notes:**
- You prioritize **south east** and the mager prioritizes **south west** due to proximity to your respective wave 21 prefires
- Do **not** ayak south if there is only **1 mage** obstructing the dinhs — this will very likely cause a double-up
- If there are **two mages on the same pillar**, always target the one that is most **east or north**

---
## Wave 22 Big — Important Note

> If neither you nor your mager popped the wave 22 big and it turns range, **do not pop it**

It does not expire until after insta 29 and leaving it alive will set you up for a better push into wave 29
