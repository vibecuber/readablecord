__**Waves 24-29 — Pushing 29 & Prepping Cleanup**__

This channel picks up immediately after the BP → Ayak in #wave-24 and covers how to push for a fast wave 29 spawn while prepping an efficient cleanup.

---
## Wave 22 Dim — Follow It Religiously

Before wave 25 spawns, strictly follow your **wave 22 dim** *(see #dims-and-expiries-overview-unfinished)* and only hit **undimmed crabs**:
- This means ignoring dimmed rangers even if there are a lot of them — they will expire very soon at this point
- Following the dim closely is critical for pushing wave 29 fast and gives your mager more freedom to pop bigs on waves 26-27

---
## Wave 25 Ranged Big

How you handle the 25 ranged big is mostly determined by how well you executed:
- #wave-19-20
- #wave-20-21 
- #wave-22 

Assuming those were executed correctly, popping the 25 big should be a straightforward decision almost every time.

**The rule:**
> Pop the 25 ranged big when you have **2-3 range smalls alive**

- This minimizes ayak time by feeding you staggered range smalls optimally
- Waiting until **zero rangers** are left before popping will result in more maging

**Exception:**
> If you are overwhelmed with smalls → ignore the 2-3 rule and pop the 25 after your 28 flickers — this will still split it in time for 3 stall 28

> Always aim to pop the **south 25** every time for a better cleanup, but be aware that the pop spot won't always be static

---|
## Extra Mage Bigs (waves 25-27)

- Popping extra mage bigs 25-27 is **risky and extremely room state dependent**
- In most scenarios you will **not** send any mage bigs here
- Pushing the waves is the ranger's primary goal — popping an extra mage big is a luxury, not a standard
> Avoid this unless you are very comfortable reading room states

---
## Wave 27 South Ranged Big

- Always aim to **first-tick** your wave 27 big — this aligns tick-perfectly with the mager's wave 26 dinhs *(see #wave-26-dinhs)*
- It is worth doing a **3-tick pipe** or skipping a full ayak to make this happen

---
## Pushing Wave 29

- You can **skip your south ranged flicker** if your mager is performing the wave 26 dinhs — it is part of their rotation
> If you are low on rangers, do **not** skip this
- Focus rangers where possible and push wave 29 into cleanup
