__**Waves 5-8**__

This channel covers how to maximize efficiency across waves 5-10 and avoid:
- Wave 6 stalls
- Wave 8 stalls
- Wave 10 stalls (indirectly)

This channel picks up immediately after killing the wave 5 range big.

---
## Wave 6 Stalls
*(Rare but avoidable)*

- **Start pathing away** from the wave 5 range big immediately after killing it to minimize travel time on your next ayak
- Fill ticks on a mage depending on how fast you killed your range big relative to how fast the mager killed their mage big

**Ayak priority order:**
```
1. Wave 3 aggro
2. Wave 5
3. Wave 2 melee aggro
```

---
## Wave 8 Stalls
*(Rare but more common than the wave 6 stall)*

There are two levels of severity:

```
Less severe  → hit more smalls
More severe  → delay the claw spec
```

**Less Severe:**
A wave 8 stall in this case is usually a sign you're moving to your wave 6/7 south rangers too early. Wait until **both** the wave 6 and wave 7 are in blowpipe range before going to them.

**Small priority while waiting:**
```
1. Rangers
2. Magers
3. Melees on the south side of the room
```

**More Severe:**
If you're already correctly hitting splits and waiting for both the 6/7 to be in blowpipe range and wave 8 is still stalling, the cause is usually the **wave 6 melee big splitting before the wave 9 check** due to an early claw spec.

**How to fix this:**

```
Wave 7 range big killed fast (2 pipes or less):
→ Add a 4-tick action between killing the wave 7 range big
  and clawing the wave 6 melee big

Wave 7 range big killed slow (3 pipes or more):
→ Claw spec the wave 6 melee big immediately after killing the wave 7
```

> The underlying cause in severe cases is that you and your partner didn't hit splits well enough prior to the claw spec — recognizing this is the first step to fixing it
