__**Wave 24 Prefire**__

This is a short channel covering awareness of the BP → Ayak prefire on the west 24 and how to execute it correctly.

---
## Why BP → Ayak?

The most optimal way to kill the wave 24 prefire is with a **blowpipe followed by an ayak**:

- Highest kill odds of any available option (approx. 4% higher than bowing)
- Doesn't off-tick you with the room
- Lets you hit **1 extra small** before the wave 25 check, reducing stall risk

---
## Mechanical Notes

- It is okay to **BP a dimmed wave 22 small** *(see #what-are-dims-and-expiries)* between the wave 23 flickers and the wave 24 BP → ayak — this BP will be your **last hit before the wave 25 check**
- If there are **no ranged smalls** available → skip the BP → ayak and use a **tbow** instead
- After the wave 23 flickers, **do not hug the barrier** — path slightly toward the middle of the room while staying close enough to return to BP range in 1 tick
> This keeps you in range of smalls that would otherwise be out of reach from the barrier

---
## Full Rotation Example

*From wave 23:*
```
Tbow 23 > pipe 23 flicker > pipe 23 flicker & step away 2 tiles >
pipe any small ranger & step back in 2 tiles > pipe 24 >
ayak 24 if alive / ayak an undimmed small mager if it died
```
