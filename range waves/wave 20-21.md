__**Waves 20-21**__

This rotation starts at the **wave 20 west prefire** and is designed to maximize your odds of not stalling wave 21.

> :warning: Refer to #surviving before attempting this rotation — you will take approximately 20 damage every time without it
> If you are struggling with pillar HP, prefire the **east** big instead on wave 20

---
## Standard Rotations

**With rangers:**
```
Tbow 20 > 3x BP > Chin 21 > BP / ayak melee distance / claw scratch
```

**Without rangers:**
```
Tbow > 2x ayak > Chin 21 > BP / ayak melee distance / claw scratch
```

---
## Chin Rotations

```
Tbow > Chin > Chin > Chin 21 > BP / ayak melee distance / claw scratch
Tbow > 2x BP > Chin > Chin 21
```

---
## Re-Chin

- If you **1st tick chin 21** you can re-chin it from right up against the barrier for it to register before the wave 22 check
- Watch your **exp drop** to do this
