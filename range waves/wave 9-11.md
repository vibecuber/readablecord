__**Waves 9-11**__

This channel covers how to maximize efficiency across waves 9-11 and avoid:
- Wave 10 stalls
- Wave 11 stalls

This channel picks up from blowpiping the wave 8 east small ranger.

---
## Wave 9 Setup

**Priority:** Kill the wave 9 range big as fast as possible without losing ticks

- After piping the wave 8 small, hit rangers as you move into blowpipe range of the **wave 9 west range big**
- If there aren't enough rangers to reach the big without losing ticks → fill with an **ayak on the wave 9 west range small**
- Any time you ayak during wave 9 you will always have enough time to path to the big without losing ticks

> Popping the wave 9 range big ASAP is critical for avoiding wave 10 stalls — splits that spawns from this big are important

After killing the big, hit whatever you can while making sure you are on time for:
```
✓ 1st tick chin wave 10
✓ 2nd or 3rd tick chin wave 10
✗ Do NOT 4th tick chin — the tile required for it to register before the wave 11 check is inconvenient
```

---
## Wave 10 Spawn — Avoiding a Wave 10 Stall

**1st tick chin:**
```
Chin > Blowpipe > Chin > Chin
```
- You have time to blowpipe anything in the room between your first and second chin
- This blowpipe registers before the wave 11 check and drastically reduces wave 10 stalls

**2nd or 3rd tick chin:**
```
Blowpipe > Chin > Chin > Chin
```
- Same concept but the blowpipe happens before the first chin, either the same tick or 1 tick before wave 10 spawns

---
## Wave 10 Into Wave 11 — Avoiding a Wave 11 Stall

After chinning the west wave 10, check how many crabs you killed:

```
0 crabs killed (didn't 1-shot) → re-chin east
1 crab killed                  → chin south to get ahead on wave 11 range smalls
2 crabs killed                 → chin east to get ahead on splits
```

> If the wave 10 range big is still alive after your chin, **clear all range smalls before popping the big** — this maximizes your chances of not stalling wave 11
