__**Vod Review — How It Works**__

This channel explains how to request a Nylo VOD review and how to get the most out of the experience.

---

**General Info:**
- VOD reviews are held in **private DMs**
- Reviews can be done via **text or voice chat**
- Your duo partner is welcome to join — in many cases it's even more beneficial
- If voice chat is used, please be respectful and avoid talking over others
- I'm **North American Central Time**, so scheduling voice reviews with EU players may be difficult
- I may be busy or tired, so please be patient

---

**How to Request a VOD Review:**

**1.** Send me a DM requesting a review

**2.** State your Nylo average
*(e.g. 321-319 / 319-316 / 316-314 / 314-312 / 312-309)*
> If you pot with multiple partners, use whichever partner gives you the **highest average**

> :warning: **Please be honest with your averages.** The goal of the VOD review is to improve your Nylo average — even toward peak times like a 302. I can't help you improve if I'm not seeing a representative run. Honesty makes the process better for both of us.

**3.** Gather **3 separate clips** of Nylos within your average
> Multiple clips help identify whether a mistake is recurring or a one-time occurrence — both are worth discussing

**4.** Send me all 3 clips

**5.** Await your VOD review
> Reviews are scaled to your average — slower averages are kept shorter to avoid overwhelming you with information, faster averages go deeper

```
321-319 → ~5-6 points of interest
319-316 → ~6-7 points of interest
316-314 → ~7-8 points of interest
314-312 → ~8-9 points of interest
312-309 → ~9-10 points of interest
```

**6.** Feel free to ask questions!
