__**Transition Into Post-Cap**__

Transition is one of the most important parts of achieving a clean and fast post-cap.

---

**What Is a Good Transition?**

A good transition refers to how many bigs you kill across waves **18, 19, and 20**. The goal is to kill:
- All **18** bigs
- All **19** bigs
- All **20** bigs

> Do **not** dwell on killing bigs after wave 20 spawns, except for the two 20s.

There are two exceptions for mage found in #wave-19-21 

---

**How Transition Affects Post-Cap**

There are subtle differences in how post-cap is played depending on the quality of your transition and your hits:
- A **good transition** can carry bad hits
- **Good hits** can carry a bad transition
- Playing for a good transition will generally give you the **best odds of a smooth post-cap**

---

**Transition Bigs — Timing Details**

When referring to transition bigs specifically, these are bigs that **split before the wave 22 dim**. Any bigs that split after the wave 22 dim will contribute to a **wave 28 stall**.
