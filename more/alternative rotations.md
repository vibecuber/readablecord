## __Staggering 20s__

---
**Changes**

- Ranger does **not** play for the 20 in lane — can pipe it if no splits are available yet
- Ranger **claws the 20 east** after their last action to push wave 22
  - If 1st tick chin 21 → minimum 1 more pipe first, maximum of 3 more pipes
- Mager **scythes the 20 west** if it is still alive after their wave 21 barrage
- Ranger **prefires 23 east** with ayak → bp prefires 23 south → still gets 23 west flickers

---
**Purpose**

- Provides very safe transitions
- Splits both 20 bigs in time to expire, helping push wave 29 out of 28 stalls
- Getting ahead on 20-21 splits creates room to pop the 23 big
