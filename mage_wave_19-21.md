__**Waves 19-21 Rotation**__

This is a 28-tick rotation split into two chunks:
```
Waves 19-20 → 12 ticks
Waves 20-21 → 16 ticks
```

**Two key rules before anything else:**
- If you ever lose ticks, readjust so that you **barrage the 21 west double mages on the 1st or 2nd tick**
- **Do NOT prefire** the 19 east with an Ayak unless it is 2 ticks late *(or 3 ticks late with a thrall out)* — earlier will cause it to split before wave 20 spawns

---
## Rotation 1: Scythe 20 Before 21

> **Do NOT prefire the wave 20 south mage big** unless there are no immediate splits or 19 mage bigs to hit — it is more consistent to kill it via scythe or macs

**Two important rules for hitting splits in this rotation:**
- When hitting **mage splits** → prioritize **south** splits
- When hitting **melee splits** → prioritize **north** splits

---

**Rotation Start** *(Wave 19 spawns)*

`Shadow 19 east mage` → `Dart 18 north ranger` → *fill ticks by hitting 19 mage bigs, mage splits, or melee splits* → `Melee the 20 south big`

> If you don't shadow the 19 east mage big on the first tick, you can skip the dart

---

**From the 20 south big, your next actions depend on what tick and what weapon you used:**

```
1st tick scythe  → Scythe 20 > ayak a split > Barrage 21 > Melee north split
2nd tick scythe  → Scythe 20 > dart or afk 2t > Barrage 21 > Melee north split
                   (skip dart if it would drag you from the barrier before the barrage)
3rd tick scythe  → Scythe 20 > afk 1t > Barrage 21 > Melee north split
4th tick scythe  → Scythe 20 > Barrage 21 > Melee north split
2nd tick sulphur → Sulphur 20 > ayak a split > Barrage 21 > Melee north split
5th tick sulphur → Sulphur 20 > Barrage 21 > Melee north split
```
‎ 
**After barraging 21:**
- Always hit a melee small **immediately** after barraging 21 on the **1st tick** — it will die before the wave 22 check
- **Do not** melee after barraging 21 on the 2nd tick
- This only works with high overkill chance weapons *(sulphur blade, macs)*

---
## Rotation 2: Scythe 20 After 21

Sometimes the room is too full and you aren't clearing enough smalls before the 21 barrage. In this case, **hold the 20 big until after the barrage.**

> This is entirely room state dependent — judge based on how many bigs and smalls are being killed

**Critical rule if holding the 20 big:**
- Scythe it **immediately** after barraging — nothing in between or it will switch to ranged

---

**Rotation Examples** *(Wave 20 spawns — 16 ticks, all end on Barrage 21 → Scythe 20)*

```
Ayak x5 > afk 1t > Barrage 21 > Scythe 20
Ayak x4 > mac/sulphur > Barrage 21 > Scythe 20
```

> These rotations can be used every time — not 100% optimal but far better than getting lost

**Timing tell:** You can tell wave 21 is about to spawn when the **20 east melee big reaches the pillar** — you can squeeze one hit in as it arrives or just before
